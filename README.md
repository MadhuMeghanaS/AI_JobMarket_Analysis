# AI Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

![Power BI](https://img.shields.io/badge/Power_BI-F2C811?style=for-the-badge&logo=powerbi&logoColor=black)

## 📌 Project Overview
The **AI Job Market Intelligence** dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the evolving landscape of artificial intelligence careers. It offers actionable insights into workforce hiring trends, required skills, and company-level intelligence to help professionals and organizations navigate the AI industry.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Data Visualization**: Microsoft Power BI
- **Data Processing**: Power Query / DAX

## 💡 Key Features
- **Hiring Trends**: Tracking the growth and demand for AI roles over time.
- **Skills Analysis**: Identifying the most sought-after technical and job titles across varies industries in the AI domain.
- **Company Insights**: Analyzing job distribution and salary comparisons by company size, evaluating remote vs on-site roles, and breaking down average salaries by experience level and location.
